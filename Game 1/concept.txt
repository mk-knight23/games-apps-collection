Title: Zombie Plant Defenders
Genre: Tower Defense / Strategy
Objective: Defend your garden from hordes of comical, dancing zombies using an arsenal of mutant plants with hilarious abilities.
Gameplay Mechanics:
- Place strategic plants in garden lanes that shoot peas, launch corn missiles, or produce toxic gas
- Each plant has unique funny animations and sound effects (e.g., disco-dancing sunflowers, farting chilis)
- Zombies come in ridiculous costumes (business suits, tutus, superhero capes) and dance their way to your house
- Power-ups include "Compost Bomb" that makes all plants temporarily giant and "Zombie Hypnosis" that makes enemies dance in place
- Progressive difficulty with boss levels featuring giant zombie bosses with silly weaknesses
Target Audience: Casual gamers aged 12-45 who enjoy humor and strategy games
Monetization Strategy:
- Free-to-play with rewarded video ads for extra plants/power-ups
- In-app purchases for special plant packs and garden decorations
- Seasonal events with exclusive zombie costumes and plant variants
