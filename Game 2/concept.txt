Title: Clumsy Cat Chaos
Genre: Physics-based Puzzle / Simulation
Objective: Help a perpetually clumsy cat navigate through increasingly absurd household environments without causing complete destruction.
Gameplay Mechanics:
- Tap and drag to guide a physics-based cat through obstacle courses filled with breakable objects
- Cat has realistic clumsy movements - trips, slides, and knocks things over with hilarious ragdoll physics
- Each level features different rooms (kitchen, living room, bathroom) with unique hazards and interactive objects
- Collect "Nine Lives" power-ups to undo catastrophic failures
- Unlock different cat breeds with unique clumsiness traits (e.g., "Slippery Paws" Siamese, "Tail Trouble" Tabby)
- Sound effects include exaggerated crashing, meowing, and human reactions of frustration/amusement
Target Audience: Cat lovers and puzzle game enthusiasts aged 10-40
Monetization Strategy:
- Free download with ads between levels
- In-app purchases for new cat breeds, costumes, and furniture packs
- "Catnip Boost" consumables for temporary grace periods
- Premium "Ad-Free Cat Paradise" version
