Title: Office Prank Wars
Genre: Multiplayer Party Game / Strategy
Objective: Out-prank your coworkers in a hilarious office environment while avoiding getting caught by the grumpy boss.
Gameplay Mechanics:
- Turn-based multiplayer where players set up elaborate prank traps on office colleagues
- Choose from various prank types: whoopee cushions, stapler in jello, fake spiders, computer mouse pranks
- Each prank has different risk/reward levels and hilarious animated reactions
- Avoid the "Boss Patrol" - a grumpy manager who walks randomly through the office
- Collect "Suspicion Points" - too many and you're sent to HR (game over)
- Power-ups include "Invisibility Cloak" (briefcase), "Distraction Device" (loud phone ring), and "Blame Shifter"
- Office environments become progressively larger and more complex (cubicles, break room, executive floor)
Target Audience: Office workers and party game enthusiasts aged 16-45
Monetization Strategy:
- Free-to-play with optional premium prank packages
- Cosmetic purchases: different office themes, character outfits, prank skins
- "Remove Ads" premium version
- Seasonal events with holiday-themed pranks and office decorations
- Team battle pass with exclusive rewards
