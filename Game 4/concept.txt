Title: Dance Battle Seniors
Genre: Rhythm / Dancing Game
Objective: Help elderly characters win dance competitions by mastering ridiculous dance moves and outperforming rival nursing homes.
Gameplay Mechanics:
- Tap, swipe, and hold patterns to the beat of popular oldies songs remixed with modern beats
- Characters include Grandma Betty with her hip replacement dance moves, Grandpa Joe doing the "Walker Shuffle"
- Each character has unique special moves: "Cane Spin," "Denture Drop," "Hearing Aid Harmony"
- Progressive difficulty from simple waltzes to complex breakdancing sequences
- Dress-up system with outrageous senior outfits ( Disco suits, hip hop gear, ballroom gowns)
- Multiplayer battles where seniors from different nursing homes compete for the "Golden Walker" trophy
- Hilarious fail animations when players miss beats - characters trip, lose teeth, or get tangled in oxygen tubes
Target Audience: All ages, especially families and players who enjoy humor and rhythm games
Monetization Strategy:
- Free with ad-supported gameplay
- In-app purchases for new characters, songs, and costume packs
- "Energy Drinks" for continues and power-ups
- VIP pass for exclusive content and ad-free experience
- Seasonal dance competitions with themed rewards
