Title: Food Fight Frenzy
Genre: Action / Arcade
Objective: Engage in epic food battles in various locations while avoiding angry chefs and cleaning staff who try to stop the chaos.
Gameplay Mechanics:
- Top-down view where players throw food items at opponents in real-time battles
- Collect different food weapons: spaghetti flingers, meatball launchers, cream pie cannons, banana peel traps
- Each food type has unique properties and damage effects (ketchup slows enemies, hot sauce burns, ice cream freezes)
- Battle locations include school cafeteria, fancy restaurant, food truck festival, and supermarket
- Dodge angry chefs with rolling pins and janitors with mops who try to clean up the mess
- Power-ups include "Super Size" (giant food portions), "Rapid Fire" (faster throwing), and "Shield" (plate as defense)
- Multiplayer modes: Free-for-all food fights, team battles, and capture the flag with food items
- Progressive unlock system with new foods, locations, and character customization
Target Audience: Casual gamers and competitive players aged 8-35 who enjoy chaotic multiplayer action
Monetization Strategy:
- Free-to-play with rewarded ads for bonus food and power-ups
- Battle pass system with seasonal food themes and exclusive rewards
- In-app purchases for cosmetic items: character outfits, food skins, and special effects
- Premium currency for faster progression and exclusive food weapons
- Tournament entry fees with real prize pools for competitive players
